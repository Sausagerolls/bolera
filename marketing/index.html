Features

Designed for people who care about their music.

Every screen is hand-built — no Catalyst, no web wrapper. Just fast, native SwiftUI that feels at home on your Mac and your phone.

Daily Mixes that actually fit

Themed playlists that change every day — Bright Morning, Throwback Thursday, Late Night Vibes — seeded from your real listening and tightened by Last.fm so everything sits in the same pocket.

A real 10-band EQ

Biquad-driven equalizer with a tap-and-drag interface and Pro presets. Tweak it once on Mac, sync the curve across all your devices via iCloud.

Artists worth visiting

Spotify-style artist pages with bios, top tracks ranked by Last.fm popularity, full album grid, and similar artists from your own library. Tap and stay a while.

Offline that just works

Download tracks, albums, or whole playlists with one tap. Bolera detects the container automatically so even FLAC and lossless ALAC files actually play offline.

Last.fm, properly wired

Sign in with your last.fm username — no API keys to register. Get curated similar artists, real bios, smarter daily mixes, and optional scrobbling that follows the official 30-second / 50% rules.

Your library, your rules

Hide the Christmas library in January. Tell Bolera to never auto-mix that one cursed album. Pin your favourites to the sidebar. Pro power-tools, opt-in only.

Design

Built like Jellyfin — finished like Apple.

Same purples, same restraint, same dark mode that gets out of the way. Vibrancy where it earns its keep. Spring-loaded animations throughout. Zero web views.

Apple Intelligence

Describe a mood. Get a playlist.

Tell Bolera what you're in the mood for and Make-a-Mix uses Apple's on-device model — augmented with Last.fm's tag data — to assemble a tonally consistent set from your own library. Nothing leaves your phone.

  • ✓ Runs on-device via Foundation Models
  • ✓ Last.fm tag→artist matching
  • ✓ Diversity-capped & decade-aware
Pro

A real 10-band EQ.

Drag-tune every band, snap to a preset, or design your own. Powered by AVFoundation's biquad chain with an MTAudioProcessingTap — so it works on streams, downloads, and AirPlay alike.

  • ✓ Live curve preview that follows the audio
  • ✓ Presets: Flat, Vocal, Bass Boost, Bright, Acoustic
  • ✓ iCloud-synced — tune once, apply everywhere
